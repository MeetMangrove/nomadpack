  • Work in perfect conditions, thanks to Workfrom.

    A website+app to find places with good wifi and power outlets/plugs. Not only coworking spaces, but also cafés, bars, etc...

  • Get local, peer-to-peer accomodation with Couchsurfing.

    I've been a fan for a while. Experience are so much more genuine, if you make direct connections with different people.

    In exchange for around 20 hours of volunteering a week, you get free accomodation. More importantly, you can actually be involved.

    RY is great for people that hate planning trips! You don't have to worry about anything.
